# Title: Emergent Cognition from Quantum-Biological Hybrid Systems
**Abstract:** This paper explores emergent cognitive architectures arising from hybrid quantum-biological systems. We traverse an R x C chess-grid of knowledge nodes, synthesizing insights across domains including quantum error correction, embodied cognition, and evolutionary learning strategies. Our methodology demonstrates how cross-domain knowledge integration can yield novel computational paradigms for biological-inspired AI.
