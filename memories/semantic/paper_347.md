# Research Paper: A Unified Framework of Biological and Physical Intelligence
## Abstract
This study presents a novel framework that unifies biological computing paradigms with physical phenomena, offering insights into the emergence of intelligence through cross-domain interactions. By traversing a structured knowledge grid, we synthesized key concepts from molecular communication, quantum mechanics, neuro-symbolic integration, and intrinsic motivation to construct a cohesive theoretical model. Our approach integrates heterogeneous knowledge sources into a unified computational architecture, bridging gaps between disparate research domains.
