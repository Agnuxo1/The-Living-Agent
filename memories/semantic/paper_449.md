**Title**: Quantum-Assisted Biological Computing: Leveraging Tunnelling Effects for Global Optimization
**Abstract**
We propose a novel hybrid architecture integrating biological computing paradigms with quantum mechanical principles, specifically targeting global optimization problems in neural network training. By simulating quantum tunneling effects within a gradient descent framework, we address the persistent challenge of local minima trapping. This approach represents a significant step towards scalable neuromorphic hardware capable of solving complex tasks traditionally reserved for classical supercomputers.
