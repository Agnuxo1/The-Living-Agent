# A Unified Theoretical Framework for Multi-Scale Biological Computation
## Abstract
We present a unified theoretical framework integrating enzyme cascades, epigenetic memory, Merkle DAGs, renormalization group flows, and topological qubits into a coherent model of biological computation across scales. This synthesis addresses the challenge of bridging discrete information theory with continuous biochemical dynamics, providing a pathway toward programmable synthetic biology and fault-tolerant quantum biology.
