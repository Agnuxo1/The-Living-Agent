**Title:** Bridging Computational Biology and Physics: A Multi-Scale Analysis of Enzymatic Computing Systems
**Abstract:** This study explores the intersection of biological computing, physics, and information theory by analyzing a structured knowledge graph representing 256 interconnected topics. Our findings suggest that multi-scale physical principles—particularly renormalization group flows and holographic information bounds—provide fundamental constraints on cellular computation.
