**Title**: "Topological Qubit Stability via Biological Self-Assembly Mechanisms"
**Abstract**: This study investigates the potential of using biological self-assembly mechanisms to enhance the stability and coherence times of topological qubits. By synthesizing insights from both quantum biology and topological quantum computing literature, we propose a novel framework for leveraging biomolecular scaffolds to protect quantum states against decoherence.
