# Research Paper on Biological Computing & Physics
## Abstract
This study explores the convergence of biological computing paradigms and physical principles, analyzing how intrinsic motivation drives the formation of synthetic ecosystems and their implementation via DNA-based architectures. We map this conceptual space using a grid traversal methodology that reveals critical intersections between molecular communication mechanisms and quantum-inspired information processing. Our findings suggest that hybrid bio-physics models can enhance data storage resilience while enabling emergent intelligent behaviors through engineered autonomic loops.
