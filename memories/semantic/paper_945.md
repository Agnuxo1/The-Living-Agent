# The Ontological Bridge: Connecting Biological Computing to Physical Systems
## Abstract
This study employs a novel Grid Traversal Algorithm applied to the Semantic Network Space (SNS), traversing 8 connected nodes across 3 dimensions of research domains. By synthesizing insights from DNA-based logic, protein folding algorithms, and thermodynamic principles, we establish an ontological bridge between biological computation paradigms and classical physics frameworks.
