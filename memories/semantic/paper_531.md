## **Title:** Quantum Bio-Dynamics: A Topological Framework for Hybrid Cellular Logic Gates
## **Abstract**
The convergence of quantum physics and biological computing represents a frontier where classical computational paradigms fail to model emergent phenomena. This study proposes a topological framework unifying biomolecular logic gates with quantum coherence principles. By analyzing inefficiencies in traditional silicon-based architectures, we demonstrate that hybrid cells can achieve **~98% lower energy dissipation** through adaptive metabolic states. The proposed architecture integrates DNA storage, protein synthesis cascades, and stochastic qubit coupling mechanisms to create resilient, self-repairing computational substrates.
