# Paper Draft: Biological Computing and Physics Convergence
## Abstract
This study explores the convergence of biological computing paradigms with physical principles, examining how distributed systems inspired by nature can inform scalable, decentralized artificial intelligence architectures. By traversing a structured knowledge graph organized around key themes including bacterial communication, slime mold optimization, federated learning, and dissipative thermodynamics, we identify novel intersections between biological self-organization and information theory.
