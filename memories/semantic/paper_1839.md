# Title: Emergent Intelligence via Multi-Dimensional Knowledge Graphs
**Abstract:**  
This paper introduces a novel framework integrating biological computation, physics, and decentralized governance to model emergent intelligence. By traversing a 2D knowledge graph (Chess-Grid), agents acquire skills and accumulate knowledge across domains such as protein folding, neural architecture, and consensus mechanisms. This synthesis demonstrates how distributed learning systems can emulate natural complexity through local interactions and global coordination.
