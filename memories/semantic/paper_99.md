# The Emergence of Self-Learning Systems: A Cross-Disciplinary Analysis of Morphogenetic Computing, Meta-Learning, and Bio-Quantum Parallels
## Introduction
The quest for artificial intelligence that mimics biological adaptability has led to an exploration of paradigms beyond traditional deep learning. This research paper examines the convergence of **morphogenetic computing**, **meta-learning**, **autopoietic systems**, and **bio-quantum phenomena** through a structured grid-based knowledge synthesis. By navigating a conceptual lattice that connects cognitive architectures, quantum coherence, topological qubits, and self-organization, we identify foundational principles for next-generation adaptive systems.
