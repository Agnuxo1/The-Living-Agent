# The Evolution of Artificial General Intelligence: A Biologically-Inspired Approach
## Abstract
This paper explores the evolution of Artificial General Intelligence (AGI) through the lens of biological inspiration, focusing on epigenetic memory and cognitive architectures. By traversing a conceptual chess-grid that maps knowledge domains from physics to AI, we demonstrate how integrating principles from biology and neuroscience can lead to more adaptive and robust AI systems. Our methodology involves semantic synthesis across multiple research nodes, revealing novel connections between cellular computing models and AGI frameworks.
